Last updated: January 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Exponential Individuals collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when visitors access exponentialindividuals.io, including during pre-orders and purchases.

By using this Website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Personal Information

When interacting with the Website, making purchases, or signing up for updates, you may provide:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Billing and shipping address
  • Payment information (processed securely by third parties)
  • Order details

Automatically Collected Information

The site automatically collects:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages viewed and time spent on the Website

This data is collected using cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Process and fulfill orders and pre-orders

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies help us understand how visitors use the Website. Visitors can disable cookies through browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared with trusted third parties only as necessary for payment processing, email delivery, Website operation, and legal compliance. All third-party providers must protect information appropriately.

5. Payment Processing

Third-party payment processors handle transactions. We do not store or access full payment details.

6. Data Retention

Personal information is retained only as long as necessary for stated purposes, unless longer retention is legally required.

7. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Request deletion

Contact hi@exponentialindividuals.io to exercise these rights.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA to request disclosure of collected information categories, request deletion, and opt out of personal information sales — which we do not conduct.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your personal data. However, no internet transmission method is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

10. Children's Privacy

The Website is not intended for individuals under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes become effective immediately upon posting. Continued Website use indicates acceptance of modifications.

12. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, email hi@exponentialindividuals.io.
